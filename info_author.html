* Author Lifetime

    We study the lifetime (the interval between the year an author first publish a paper and the year he/she publish for last time).


    Note that, due to current time, many of the authors have publications up to now (2019). Following figure shows the active career time of six most productive authors.

    The maximum lifetime is 20 years, i.e., those pioneers in NoC; however, these long lifetime authors are few. Most of the authors have a lifetime of only 1 or 2 years.

    The reason is that, these authors are students, and they do not remain in the academia after publishing one or two papers and graduated.



* Author Subjects

   In early days, new authors chose flow control, emerging technology, and NoC for memory.

    The maximum lifetime is 20 years, i.e., those pioneers in NoC; however, these long lifetime authors are few. Most of the authors have a lifetime of only 1 or 2 years.

    However, more recently, fewer people are choosing these subjects. Instead, NoC for SiP (a.k.a, chiplet systems), routing and NoC for different applications are chosen by more new authors, which reflects the frontier of the NoC area.
